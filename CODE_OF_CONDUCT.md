# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and discuss ideas, not people.
- Give and receive feedback professionally.
- Focus on collaboration and technical clarity.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, intimidation, or sustained disruption
- Publishing private information without consent

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to:

- Email: `conduct@softos.dev`

All reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially where possible.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warning,
temporary restriction, or removal from the community.
